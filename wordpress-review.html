FAQs

Is Wordpress paid monthly?

Wordpress can be paid monthly or by year. The platform will only work through a web hosting provider, so take your hosting payment plan (monthly or anual) as the reference. Apart from your web hosting service, Wordpress doesn't need any other payment to run.

How much traffic can a Wordpress website handle?

Your Wordpress website performance is directly proportional to you web hosting service capabilities, so your site will suport as much traffic as you hosting plan indicates.

What can you build with Wordpress?

With Wordpress you can various different types of websites: Landing pages, blogs/online magazine, online stores (woocommerce), portfolio websites, business websites...

Is Wordpress good for beginners?

Yes. The Wordpress platform is a very effective non-coding approach to create a website

Does Wordpress require coding?

No. Wordpress doesnt require that you do any coding to make it work.
