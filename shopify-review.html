FAQs

Is Shopify good for beginners?

Yes. You dont require any previous design/coding/online marketing technical skills to get the ball rolling. Shopify gives you templates, intuitive platform functionality, and the marketing integrations that allows you to successfully position you brand online.

What companies use Shopify?

Some of the brands that use Shopify: Gymshark, Heinz, The Economist, Hasbro, Red Bull, MVMT, Penguin Books...

How secure is Shopify?

Yes, Shopify is very secure both for customers and for the online store owner. Shopify is powered by PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) and all its websites are PCI DSS compliant by default

Where can I sell with Shopify?

You can sell anywhere in the world as long as you, as the store owner have a defined distribution framework; shipping, local delivery, and local pickup.
